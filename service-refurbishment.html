Our Methodology

The Refurbishment Journey

A rigorous multi-step process that ensures every device meets quality standards

01

Device Assessment

Comprehensive hardware and software diagnostic. Each device is tested for functionality, cosmetic condition, and component integrity. Assessment report generated.

02

Data Sanitization

NIST-certified data destruction on all storage media. Multiple overwrite passes, degaussing for magnetic media, and certificate of erasure issued.

03

Component Repair & Replacement

Faulty components replaced with genuine or OEM-equivalent parts. RAM upgrades, battery replacement, thermal paste renewal, and keyboard repair.

04

Deep Cleaning

Professional ultrasonic cleaning of circuit boards, compressed air dust removal, chassis reconditioning, and screen polishing for cosmetic restoration.

05

Quality Testing & Burn-in

48-hour stress testing, performance benchmarking, temperature monitoring, and full I/O port verification. Only passing devices proceed to certification.

06

Certification & Warranty

VOZON certification label applied, 6-month warranty issued, and detailed condition report provided. Asset tagging available for inventory management.
