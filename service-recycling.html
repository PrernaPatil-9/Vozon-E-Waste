Step by Step

Our Recycling Process

A rigorous, certified process that ensures maximum recovery and zero environmental harm

01

Intake & Weighing

All incoming e-waste is logged, weighed, and catalogued. Each client batch is tracked through our system with a unique ID.

02

AI-Powered Sorting

Smart visual classification segregates devices by type, material composition, and hazard level for optimal routing.

03

Data Destruction

NIST-certified degaussing, shredding, and multi-pass overwriting ensures 100% data sanitization before any processing.

04

Precision Dismantling

Certified technicians manually dismantle devices following strict safety protocols to maximize component recovery.

05

Material Separation

Advanced mechanical and chemical processes separate metals, plastics, glass, and rare earth elements.

06

Certificate & Report

Clients receive recycling certificates, weight reports, CO₂ savings data, and ESG documentation.
